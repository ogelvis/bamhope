About BAM-HOPE

BAM-HOPE Integrated Nigeria Limited is a fully Nigerian-owned oil and gas service company. Our primary focus is to deploy specialized technical expertise and motivate our team of professionals to contribute positively to the Nigerian Petroleum and Energy industry, particularly in West Africa.

Our Vision & Values

Our vision is to be the best at what we do, working together, across boundaries, to meet the needs of our customers, clients, shareholders and associates to help the company win. We create a “can do” spirit. We do the right thing in a reliable way, creating value both for our business and for our communities. We demonstrate honest, ethical behaviour in everything we do. We do not rest on our accomplishments, but rather build on them to be the best we can.

Our Mission

To operate efficiently and sustainably across the oil and gas value chain, building on long-term relationships with clients and partners to deliver exceptional results for our communities and stakeholders.
